Our Story

At SpaceIT, we are committed to helping astronauts navigate the challenges of space by offering a seamless blend of inventory tracking and health management. Our software ensures that supplies are efficiently monitored and health is carefully maintained, providing critical support in the vast expanse of space.

In the demanding and isolating environment of space, where every decision can have significant consequences, SpaceIT becomes an indispensable tool for astronauts. Our system not only tracks vital resources in real time, preventing shortages and reducing waste, but it also manages health appointments, to ensure astronauts' health is never sidelined. This comprehensive approach allows astronauts to maintain their physical and mental well-being while ensuring that their mission remains on track. By reducing the burden of resource management and health monitoring, SpaceIT enables astronauts to focus fully on their mission objectives, confident in the knowledge that their health and resources are meticulously managed.
